The smoothie is a fruit or vegetable-based diet drink ("smooth" means light), and is a concentrate of vitamins with a thick consistency served with ice cubes. The nutrition of a smoothie depends on its ingredients and their proportions. Many smoothies include large or multiple servings of fruits and vegetables, which are recommended in a healthy diet and intended to be a meal replacement.
